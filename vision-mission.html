Group Vision & Mission

CREINTORS

Vision

To deliver sustainable innovations that build a better world and create an ecosystem that inspires the youth.

Mission

To collaborate with all our stakeholders by relying on our technical expertise and unique holistic approach, that delivers business excellence and motivates youth.

Company-Wise Vision & Mission

CTPL

Vision

To be a trusted partner for delivering unique testing solutions for various applications as per the established standards and also be a part of an ecosystem that defines these standards.

Mission

Through the culture of innovation and collaboration of people and technologies, building an ecosystem of offerings, that deliver testing facilities for encouraging every product and service provider, to test its offerings for its very purpose and thus building the trust and confidence in every end user.

CASPL

Vision

To be the customer's top choice as an innovation partner, recognized as a holistic automation architect and integrator, addressing all their end-to-end business needs of information flow and material flow solutions, delivering elevated productivity through a human centric value-addition approach, while ensuring sustainability is ingrained in every action we take.

Mission

Through the culture of innovation and collaboration of people and technologies, building an ecosystem of offerings, that deliver sustainable solutions, driven by immediate productivity gains and supported by a mechanism of continuous improvement facilitated by tools designed to significantly reduce human physical and mental fatigue, with tools enabling proactive thinking for the establishment of systems that consistently deliver business excellence.

CESPL

Vision

To be a beacon of innovation for the holistic architecture and integration of drinking water, effluent, sewage and solid waste management systems, that deliver sustainable solutions with a carbon-negative impact.

Mission

Through the culture of innovation and collaboration of people and technologies, building an ecosystem of offerings, that deliver sustainable solutions addressing the pains for assuring quality water generation and distribution with accountability, for treating effluent, sewage and solid waste by being closest to the point of generation of waste thus creating the sense of awareness and participation.
